Introduction to Crimes in Chicago

This dataset reflects reported incidents of crime (with the exception of murders where data exists for each victim) that occurred in the City of Chicago from 2001 to 2017. Data is extracted from the Chicago Police Departments CLEAR (Citizen Law Enforcement Analysis and Reporting) system. In order to protect the privacy of crime victims, addresses are shown at the block level only and specific locations are not identified.

Link to the data

https://www.kaggle.com/currie32/crimes-in-chicago#Chicago_Crimes_2012_to_2017.csv
